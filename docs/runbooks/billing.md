# Billing operations

## Trial expiry

- Day 7: email + in-app banner
- Day 11: WhatsApp / Telegram nudge
- Day 13: sales alert (prioritize this lead)
- Day 14 + 3-day grace: read-only mode + rescue email with 20% discount

## Dunning (failed payment)

- Attempt 1: immediate
- Attempt 2: +3 days
- Attempt 3: +7 days
- After 10 days: subscription cancelled, data retained for 90 days

## Refund

- 7-day money-back guarantee on first month
- Manually processed by finance via Stripe Dashboard; audit entry auto-created
