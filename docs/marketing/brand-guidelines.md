# Clary brand guidelines

## Name

- **Clary** (stylized as plain sans-serif, no logo mark before the word)
- Pronunciation: /ˈklɛɹi/ (rhymes with "hairy")

## Palette

- Primary: Clary Blue `#2563EB`
- Accent: Mint Green `#10B981`
- Dark BG: `#0A0A0A`
- Neutral scale: Zinc 50..950

## Typography

- Display + body: Geist Sans (Vercel) — weights 400, 500, 600, 700
- Code: Geist Mono — weight 400

## Voice

- Clinical where it matters (accuracy, compliance wording)
- Warm and concrete everywhere else ("Bemor ma'lumotlari bir joyda", not "Leverage our holistic patient data ecosystem")
- No corporate jargon

## Logo

- Wordmark "clary" lowercase, weight 700, letter-spacing -0.03em
- Optional mark: a stylized stethoscope-circle (TODO — contract to designer or vectorize from Figma)
- Minimum size: 24px height
