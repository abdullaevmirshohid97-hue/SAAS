# Security Disclosure Policy

We appreciate security researchers who responsibly disclose vulnerabilities.

## Scope

- `*.clary.uz` (excluding `grafana`, `glitchtip`, `dokploy` which are internal)
- `github.com/abdullaevmirshohid97-hue/SAAS`
- Clary mobile apps (iOS + Android production builds)

## Out of scope

- Social engineering
- DoS / volumetric attacks
- Third-party services (Supabase, Stripe, Cloudflare) — report to their programs

## Safe harbor

If you:

- Make a good-faith effort to avoid privacy violations, data destruction, or service disruption
- Report to security@clary.uz and give us 90 days before public disclosure
- Do not exploit the vulnerability beyond proof of concept

...we will not pursue legal action.

## Preferred disclosure

- Email: security@clary.uz (PGP key at <https://clary.uz/.well-known/pgp-key.asc>)
- Include: description, reproduction steps, impact assessment

## Rewards

We pay bounties for valid reports at our discretion:

| Severity | Bounty         |
|----------|----------------|
| Critical | $500-$2,000    |
| High     | $200-$500      |
| Medium   | $50-$200       |
| Low      | Clary swag     |
