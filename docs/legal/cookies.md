# Cookie Policy

**Effective date: 2026-04-23**

## 1. Essential cookies (always on)

- Session cookie for authentication
- CSRF protection cookie
- Preferences cookie (language, theme) — local storage, not a cookie

## 2. Analytics (cookieless)

- PostHog is configured in server-side, cookieless mode
- No cross-site tracking
- No advertising cookies

## 3. Your choices

You can disable cookies in your browser. Essential cookies are required for the Services to function.

## 4. Third-party services that may set cookies

- **Cloudflare Turnstile** — anti-bot challenge on signup/contact forms (no user tracking)
- **Stripe** (billing flows) — session cookie for Checkout

No other third-party cookies are set without your consent.
