# ADR-016: Licensing model

- Status: Accepted

## Decision

- **Code**: Proprietary / All Rights Reserved (see [LICENSE](../../LICENSE))
- **Usage**: Granted to clinics via Terms of Service + valid subscription
- **Employee/contractor IP**: Assigned to Clary LLC via separate IP Assignment Agreement (IPAA)
- **User-facing documents** (8 total): Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, DPA, Cookie Policy, SLA, AUP, Security Disclosure, EULA — all in 7 languages, reviewed by Uzbek counsel

## Consequences

- Closed-source commercial SaaS
- Some utility packages may later be open-sourced under MIT (e.g. `packages/payments/providers/click`)
- Legal costs: $300-800 one-time for counsel review
